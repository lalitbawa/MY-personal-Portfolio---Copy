What works

1. If you click on Men on top navbar, it will take to you the men's pants section. 2. If you click on the hamburger menu on the right hand side, you can click on men topwear and select tshirts or click on men bottom wear and select pants. 3. If you open the website on mobile, you can use the carousel menu which is created from scratch. 4. If you click on the filters button on tshirt or pants page, a menu will slide open from the left hand side in which you can select and use filters accordingly. all filter buttons work except reset filter button. 5. You can click on the product image to view more details about that product.
